Introduction To De-Addiction

"Prevent You" has been visionary in the concept of service to mankind with its various initiatives. Providing deaddiction and Rehabiliatation to patients suffering from alcohol use disorder (alcohol dependence/alcoholism) and nicotine addiction is another such initiative.

Alcohol and Tobacco are one of the worst chemicals that cause many serious and terminal illnesses. One such disease associated with their use is addiction. It is known since last 80 years that addiction is a chronic and progressive illness. the concept has been endorsed by World Health Organization.

Are you Suffering or not ?

How to Know Whether you are Suffering From Alcoholism or not?


  • Social withdrawal.
  • Unusual mood changes.
  • Poor physical hygiene.
  • Eyes that are red or watery.
